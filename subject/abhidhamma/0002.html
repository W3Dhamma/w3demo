Introduction to Abhidhamma

What is Abhidhamma?

Abhidhamma, which means "higher doctrine," is the third basket of the Tipitaka, the canonical scriptures of Buddhism. It offers a detailed psychological and philosophical analysis of the nature of reality.

Importance of Abhidhamma

Abhidhamma provides a framework for understanding the mind, consciousness, and the ultimate nature of existence. It is crucial for deep insight meditation and the development of wisdom.

Key Concepts in Abhidhamma

  • Citta (consciousness)
  • Cetasika (mental factors)
  • Rupa (matter)
  • Nibbana (the ultimate reality)

These concepts form the foundation of Abhidhamma philosophy and are essential for understanding the Buddhist view of reality.
